1. G12 Labs

3. How It Works

6. Security

We take no shortcuts on security. Each vault is built on protocols audited by the best firms in the sector, governed by multi-signatures, and continuously monitored. Zero risk does not exist in DeFi — but we do everything to minimise it.

Non-Custodial

Your funds remain in your wallet. We can never access them. Even if G12 Labs disappears, you keep your tokens.

Multi-Signature

No critical operation can be performed by a single person. Multiple signatures are always required.

Audited Protocols

We only use protocols verified by the world's best security firms (ChainSecurity, OpenZeppelin, Trail of Bits).

Security Delay

Any major change is announced in advance. You have time to withdraw your funds if you disagree.

24/7 Monitoring

Our systems continuously monitor positions and alert the team if a problem is detected.

Full Transparency

Everything is publicly verifiable. Every transaction, every position, every fee.

Multi-Layer Architecture

1
Enzyme Finance — Audited vault infrastructure (5+ years, $200M+ TVL, zero major exploits)
2
Multi-Signature (2-of-3) — All critical actions require at least 2 out of 3 signatories
3
Whitelisted Protocols Only — Morpho, Aave V3, Uniswap V3. No unvetted integrations.
4
Automated Monitoring — Real-time alerts, health factor tracking, LP range monitoring
5
Human Oversight — All significant decisions validated by the team before execution

9. Risks

Investing in crypto involves risks. We take them very seriously and do everything to minimise them, but zero risk does not exist.

Risk Plain explanation
Market risk Crypto prices can vary significantly. The BTC vault is directly impacted. The Stable Yield vault has low exposure.
Technical risk The protocols we use could have a bug, even though they are audited by the best firms.
Yield risk The yields shown are targets, not guarantees. They vary depending on market conditions.
Liquidity risk Temporary inability to withdraw funds (72h delay for BTC vault). In exceptional cases, positions may take longer to unwind.
Regulatory risk Laws around crypto can evolve and impact the services available.
USDC depeg risk USDC could temporarily lose its parity with the US dollar.
The yield ranges mentioned in this document (4–8% for the Stable Yield vault, 10–20% for the BTC Yield vault) are purely indicative, based on historical observations, and do not in any way constitute a promise, forecast, or guarantee of future yield. Actual yield may be lower, zero, or negative.
Security is never absolute. We prioritise it above yield. Only invest what you can afford to lose.
